What's behind Door Number 2?

You decide to approach the heavy metal door. With some effort you manage to push it open just enough so you can slip inside.

Once you get past the door, you find yourself in a dark room with tall shelves on either side of you. On the shelves are laptops and other electronic devices - some with flashing LED lights. Boxes and bins are stacked on the ground before you, and there are a few electrical cables crossing back and forth. You realize quickly that you must tread lightly.

"This must be the computer server room or something," you thought.

In the middle of the room is a long, narrow corridor leading to a storage closet with a bright white light emitting from it.
