What's that smell?

You work up the guts to investigate the funky smelling chair. The smell gets stronger as you get closer...

Plugging your nose and expecting the worst, you spin the chair around and are shocked to see the source of the smell is... old running shoes. *Phew*

"How can someone let their shoes get so smelly?!" you asked outloud to yourself.

Beside the running shoes you also see a lunchbox, a bag of gym clothes, and a set of keys.
